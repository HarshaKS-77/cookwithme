Cuisine :Mexican
Servings : 3
Mode of Cooking : Easy
Preparation Time : 15
Calorie Per Servings : 380
Cooking Time :25

Ingredients Needed

  1. Chicken thighs
  2. Mango, diced
  3. Red onion, finely chopped
  4. Cilantro, chopped
  5. Lime juice
  6. Jalapeño, minced
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
  8. Cooked rice for serving

Cooking Instruction

  1. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or bake chicken until fully cooked.
  3. In a bowl, combine diced mango, chopped red onion, cilantro, minced jalapeño, and lime juice.
  4. Dice the cooked chicken and mix it with the mango salsa.
  5. Serve over cooked rice.
